Overview

Altrastreams is committed to protecting your privacy and personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our internet services and visit our website.

Information We Collect

  • Name, address, and phone number for service setup

  • Government-issued identification for service verification
  • Technical support communications and service history

Usage Information

  • Network usage data and bandwidth consumption
  • Device information and connection logs
  • Service performance metrics and diagnostic data
  • Website analytics and interaction data

Technical Information

  • IP addresses and network identifiers
  • Equipment performance and configuration data

How We Use Your Information

  • Service Delivery: Provide, maintain, and improve our internet services
  • Customer Support: Respond to inquiries and resolve technical issues
  • Billing: Process payments and manage your account
  • Network Management: Monitor and optimize network performance
  • Security: Protect against fraud, abuse, and unauthorized access
  • Compliance: Meet legal and regulatory requirements
  • Communication: Send service updates and important notifications

Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your browsing experience and analyze website performance.

Types of Cookies

  • Essential Cookies: Required for basic website functionality
  • Performance Cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with our site
  • Functional Cookies: Remember your preferences and settings

You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings, but disabling certain cookies may affect website functionality.

Information Sharing with Third Parties

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information only in these limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Trusted partners who assist with billing, technical support, and service delivery
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or government request
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets
  • Emergency Situations: To protect the safety and security of our customers or network
  • Consent: When you explicitly authorize us to share specific information

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption of sensitive data in transit and at rest
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Access controls and employee training on data protection
  • Secure data centers with physical and digital safeguards
  • Incident response procedures for potential security breaches

While we strive to protect your information, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security but are committed to maintaining the highest standards of protection.
